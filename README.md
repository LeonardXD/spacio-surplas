Spacio Surplas is an online marketplace that serves as a digital platform 
connecting small furniture sellers with buyers who are seeking affordable and 
space-efficient home furnishings. Unlike traditional furniture stores, Spacio 
Surplas does not purchase or store inventory; instead, it allows sellers to 
upload photos, item dimensions, and prices of their surplus furniture, 
providing them with a convenient way to reach more customers without 
having to manage their own e-commerce systems or stock. Sellers can provide 
simplified 3D views or multiple-angle images for large furniture items such as 
sofas, beds, or dining tables, while smaller items are displayed using standard 
photos. For buyers, the platform enables a space-conscious shopping 
experience. They can input their room or available space dimensions, which 
allows the system to filter and display furniture items that will fit their homes, 
helping to prevent incorrect purchases and ensuring that the selected furniture 
is practical for their living space. Buyers can browse items, select their 
preferred furniture, and place an order directly through the platform. The 
system then sends notifications to sellers and provides buyers with order 
status updates such as Pending, Shipped, and Delivered, streamlining 
communication and tracking throughout the transaction. 
 
To encourage participation, Spacio Surplas offers sellers a 7–14 day free trial, 
allowing them to try the platform without initial costs. Buyers can also benefit 
from promotional and seasonal discounts, making the platform attractive to a 
wide range of users. Revenue is generated through a monthly subscription fee 
for sellers and a commission on each successful transaction, enabling the 
venture to operate with minimal financial risk while maintaining sustainability. 
Overall, Spacio Surplas provides a practical, user-friendly, and organized 
solution for both sellers and buyers, simplifying the process of selling and 
purchasing surplus furniture. By combining a centralized online marketplace 
with features like space-based filtering, optional 3D views, and order 
management, Spacio Surplas ensures that sellers can reach a broader audience 
and buyers can make informed decisions when selecting furniture for their 
homes.
